Guarantee

30-Day Speaking Progress Guarantee

Star Speaker is built to create visible speaking progress. But progress requires participation.

If the student completes at least 70% of daily practice, attends at least 70% of scheduled sessions, and completes the final assessment, but we cannot show measurable improvement in speaking confidence, answer length, fluency, or response speed, they receive extra support.

Star: 14-Day Progress Extension Super Star: 30-Day Progress Extension

FAQ

Questions before you apply.

Is this a normal English course?

No. It is a speaking training system built around daily output, correction, pressure simulation, and measurable progress.

Do I need to be advanced to join?

No. A2, B1, B2, C1, and not-sure students can apply. The free speaking analysis helps us understand fit.

What happens in the Free English Speaking Analysis?

We look at how you answer, where you hesitate, and what kind of support would help most.

Is Turkish explanation available?

Yes. Turkish explanation is available when it helps you understand the system and feedback clearly.

What if I miss a few days?

A few missed days can be managed. Missing more than 30% of the required work affects the progress guarantee.

What is the difference between Star and Super Star?

Super Star includes more private sessions, priority feedback, more group practice, and deeper weekly pressure simulation.

When does the next cohort start?

Cohorts start at the beginning of each month and are limited to 20 students.

How does payment work?

Payment details are confirmed during the consultation after your Free English Speaking Analysis. Your place is only confirmed after you accept the program terms and payment plan.
